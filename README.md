This Project takes a look at a dataset related to Germans and their credit information. It contains their ages, which we look at the average and the ranges in our analysis, the targets the credits were used and
their current situation considering the risk.

Part of the project visualizes the distribution of data in the dataset based on different factors, including age, credit amount, and so on.

We have checked the correlation among different features in the dataset, and using different plots and different visualization we have analyzed the relationship and distributions of different features.
